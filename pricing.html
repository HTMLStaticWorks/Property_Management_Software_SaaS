Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day fully featured free trial on all our plans. No credit card required to sign up.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time right from your dashboard settings.

Are there setup fees?

No! All our self-serve plans have $0 setup fees. Enterprise plans may include a custom onboarding fee depending on the complexity of data migration.

How is my data secured?

We use bank-level 256-bit AES encryption to protect all your sensitive financial and tenant data. Our servers are hosted on AWS with strict access controls.
