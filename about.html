Our Story

Built by property managers, for property managers. Discover how we're transforming the real estate industry through innovative software.

The Beginning

From Frustration to Innovation

In 2018, our founders were managing a portfolio of 500+ units. Frustrated by the fragmented tools, lost spreadsheets, and endless text messages, they realized the industry needed a better way.

PropManage was born out of a desire to create a single, unified platform that handles every aspect of property management seamlessly. Today, we empower thousands of landlords worldwide to operate more efficiently.

Our Mission

To eliminate the friction of property management by providing intuitive, automated software that helps landlords grow their portfolios while delivering exceptional tenant experiences.

Our Vision

To become the global standard operating system for real estate investors, fostering transparent, efficient, and profitable property management for generations to come.

Our Journey

Company Growth Timeline

2018

The Foundation

PropManage was founded in Austin, Texas. We launched our beta version to a closed group of 50 local property managers.

2020

Series A & Expansion

Secured $15M in Series A funding. Expanded our feature set to include automated maintenance dispatch and API integrations.

2022

Global Reach

Crossed the milestone of 5,000 active portfolios managed. Opened our first international office in London.

2026

Enterprise Enterprise

Launched the Enterprise tier for institutional investors, managing over $50B in real estate assets globally.
